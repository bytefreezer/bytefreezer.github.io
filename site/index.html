    The Problem: Post-Breach Forensics

    When a breach is discovered weeks later, you need to reconstruct what happened

    :material-alert-circle:

    Traditional SIEMs Are Expensive

    Enterprise SIEM solutions cost $1M+ annually. Splunk charges $150/GB, Datadog has complex per-host pricing. These costs make comprehensive logging prohibitive.

    :material-lock:

    Vendor Controls Your Data

    Your security data lives in vendor infrastructure. You pay for retention, pay for queries, pay for exports. When you want to leave, migration is painful and expensive.

    :material-calendar-remove:

    Limited Retention Windows

    Cost-prohibitive retention means you can only afford 7-30 days. When sophisticated attacks are discovered months later, the evidence is already gone.

    The Solution: Security DVR

    Think security camera DVR, but for all your operational data

    :material-database:

    BYOB: Bring Your Own Bucket

    • Data written directly to your S3-compatible storage
    • AWS S3, MinIO, Backblaze B2, Cloudflare R2, Wasabi
    • You control it. You own it. No vendor markup.
    • Perfect for compliance: HIPAA, SOC2, PCI-DSS, GDPR
    :material-robot:

    BYOA: Bring Your Own Agent

    • Just add your API key: OpenAI, Claude, Gemini, local LLMs
    • ByteFreezer provides integration layer (metadata, optimization, context)
    • Ask questions in natural language, get answers from complete history
    • Switch AI providers anytime - no vendor lock-in
    :material-calendar-check:

    Flexible Everything

    • Retention: 7, 30, 90, 365 days, or perpetual
    • Storage: S3 Standard ($4/TB) or Glacier ($0.40/TB)
    • Format: Standard Parquet - query with any tool
    • Leave ByteFreezer anytime - data stays accessible forever
